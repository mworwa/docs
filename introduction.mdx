**Gardian: Your Comprehensive Content Moderation Solution**

Gardian helps you moderate and detect specific content on your platform efficiently and accurately. By leveraging custom criteria, you can manage and filter any type of content according to your unique requirements.

With Gardian, you can:

- **Automate Content Moderation:** Streamline your content moderation process by reducing reliance on human moderators, allowing for 24/7 moderation without fatigue or human error.
- **Custom AI Agents:** Set up tailored AI agents designed to detect and address your specific business cases, ensuring that your platformâ€™s content aligns with your policies and standards.
- **Ready-Made Agents:** Utilize our pre-configured agents to handle common content issues:
  - Toxicity Detection: Identify and mitigate harmful or abusive language.
	- Sentiment Analysis: Assess the emotional tone of content to gauge user sentiment.
	- Propriety Checks: Ensure content adheres to appropriate standards and guidelines.
	- Spam Detection: Automatically detect and filter out spam and irrelevant content.
	- Discrimination Monitoring: Detect discriminatory language or behavior to foster an inclusive community.
	- Self-Harm and Violence Detection: Identify content related to self-harm or violence to ensure user safety.
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** Gain real-time insights and updates on the content being posted on your platform, enabling prompt action on inappropriate or harmful content.
- **Scalable Solutions:** Easily scale your moderation efforts as your platform grows, ensuring consistent and comprehensive content oversight regardless of the volume of user-generated content.
